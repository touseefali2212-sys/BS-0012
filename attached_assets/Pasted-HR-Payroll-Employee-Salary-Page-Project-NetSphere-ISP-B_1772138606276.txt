HR & Payroll – Employee Salary Page
Project: NetSphere ISP Billing SaaS
________________________________________
🎯 Objective
Design and build a professional, attractive, enterprise-level Salary Processing page.
This page must allow:
•	Monthly salary generation
•	Auto salary calculation
•	Advance & loan deduction
•	Attendance-based adjustments
•	Bonus & commission inclusion
•	Tax deduction handling
•	Approval workflow
•	Payroll finalization
•	Accounting integration
The page must feel:
✔ Financially secure
✔ Clean and structured
✔ Corporate-grade
✔ Executive-friendly

🔄 Salary Calculation Logic (System Requirements)
When “Process Payroll” is clicked:
System must automatically:
1.	Fetch base salary
2.	Calculate attendance-based deduction
3.	Add overtime
4.	Add bonus & commission
5.	Deduct loan installments
6.	Apply tax rules
7.	Calculate net salary
8.	Create payroll entry
9.	Generate accounting journal entry
Example Journal Entry:
Dr: Salary Expense Account
Cr: Employee Payable Account
On salary payment:
Dr: Employee Payable
Cr: Bank / Cash
