HR & Payroll – Employee Types & Roles Management Page

Project: NetSphere ISP Billing & ERP

🎯 Objective

Design and develop a professional, enterprise-level Employee Types & Roles Management page.

The page must allow administrators to:

Create and manage Employee Types

Define Organizational Roles

Configure Salary Structure per Type

Assign Role-based permissions

Link commission eligibility

Define payroll rules

Control access to system modules

Support multi-branch hierarchy

This page acts as the foundation of HR + Payroll + Access Control.
