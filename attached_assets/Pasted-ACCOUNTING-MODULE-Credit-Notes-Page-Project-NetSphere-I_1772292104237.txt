🔄 System Logic

When Credit Note is Approved:

✔ Reverse revenue (Debit Revenue)
✔ Reduce customer receivable
✔ Update AR aging
✔ Reflect in Trial Balance
✔ Update P&L
✔ Update Customer Outstanding

If Refund selected:

✔ Credit Cash/Bank
✔ Close credit note

If Applied to Invoice:

✔ Reduce invoice balance
✔ Update customer ledger
