TRANSACTIONS MODULE
Corporate Customer Collections Page

Project: NetSphere ISP Billing & Enterprise ERP

🎯 Objective

Develop a Corporate Customer Collections Management Page to efficiently track, manage, and control payment collections from corporate clients.

This module ensures:

✔ Structured invoice-level collection
✔ Corporate credit control
✔ Overdue monitoring
✔ Recovery officer tracking
✔ Automatic accounting integration
✔ Contract & billing compliance

This page is designed for serious B2B revenue management.

🔄 System Logic

When payment is recorded:

✔ Reduce invoice balance
✔ Update corporate outstanding
✔ Update AR aging
✔ Update credit utilization
✔ Create accounting journal entry
✔ Update dashboard KPIs

If credit exceeds limit:

✔ Trigger alert
✔ Notify finance manager

If fully paid:

✔ Remove suspension
✔ Update account to Active
