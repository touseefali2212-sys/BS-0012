ACCOUNTING MODULE
Add New Account & Account List Page

Project: NetSphere ISP Billing & Enterprise ERP

🎯 Objective

Build a professional “Add New Account” page and “Account List” management page inside the Accounting module.

This page must allow Finance/Admin to:

Create individual ledger accounts

Assign them under Account Types

Manage financial balances

Link accounts to system modules

Control posting permissions

View account balances in real-time

This is the Chart of Accounts (Operational Level).

🔄 System Logic

When account is created:

✔ It becomes available in Journal Entry
✔ It appears in Trial Balance
✔ It affects P&L or Balance Sheet based on type
✔ Opening balance auto-creates journal entry
✔ Cannot delete if transactions exist
