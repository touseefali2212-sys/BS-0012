INVENTORY MODULE
Inventory List – Batch & Serial Tab Page

Project: NetSphere ISP Billing & Enterprise ERP

🎯 Objective

Develop a dedicated Batch & Serial Management Tab within the Inventory List Page to enable precise tracking of serialized and batch-based products across warehouses, customers, and transactions.

This page ensures:

✔ Individual item traceability
✔ Batch-level control
✔ Warranty tracking
✔ Expiry monitoring
✔ Asset-customer linkage
✔ Movement history visibility

It transforms inventory into a fully traceable system.

🔄 Automation & Business Logic

When Purchase Order received:

✔ Batch auto-created
✔ Serials auto-registered
✔ Warranty start date saved

When Sales Invoice created:

✔ Specific serial must be selected
✔ Status changes to Sold
✔ Assigned to customer

When Asset assigned:

✔ Serial linked to customer account
✔ Allocation updated

When Return processed:

✔ Serial status updated
✔ Re-enter stock (if approved)

When Warranty expired:

✔ Auto flag as out-of-warranty

If serial already sold:

❌ Cannot sell again
❌ Cannot allocate
❌ Locked for editing
