INVENTORY MODULE
Stock Management Page

Project: NetSphere ISP Billing & Enterprise ERP

🎯 Objective

Develop a centralized Stock Management Page to monitor, control, and manage inventory quantities across all warehouses, POPs, and storage locations in real time.

This page ensures:

✔ Accurate stock visibility
✔ Multi-location inventory control
✔ Stock movement tracking
✔ Loss & shrinkage prevention
✔ Automated reorder alerts
✔ Financial stock valuation

It is the operational backbone of your entire supply chain.

6️⃣ Reorder & Alert System

Automated stock intelligence:

When stock ≤ Reorder Level:

✔ Trigger low-stock alert
✔ Notify procurement
✔ Suggest reorder quantity
✔ Generate draft Purchase Order

If stock below minimum:

✔ Mark as Critical
✔ Highlight in dashboard

Forecasting Logic:

Based on:

Average monthly usage

Sales trend

Allocation trend

Historical demand

🔄 Automation Logic

When PO received:

✔ Increase stock
✔ Update inventory value

When product assigned to customer:

✔ Reduce available stock

When transferred:

✔ Reduce source warehouse
✔ Increase destination warehouse

When adjustment approved:

✔ Update stock balance
✔ Log financial impact

When product discontinued:

✔ Prevent new entries
