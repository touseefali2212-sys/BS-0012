NetSphere – Bandwidth-Based Reseller Management & Accounting Module
(Professional Replit AI Description)
🎯 Objective

Develop an advanced Bandwidth-Based Reseller Management & Accounting System for ISP operations, where the company:

✔ Purchases bandwidth in bulk from upstream vendors
✔ Manages a centralized internal bandwidth pool
✔ Distributes balance-based services to resellers
✔ Tracks real-time profitability, usage, and financial performance

👉 This system replaces traditional vendor panel dependency with a fully controlled internal ISP distribution model.

🧠 Core Concept

Unlike standard reseller systems:

❌ No direct vendor package reselling
✅ Full internal control of bandwidth + pricing + distribution

The system operates on:

Bandwidth Purchase → Internal Pool → Reseller Balance → Customer Services → Revenue Tracking
🧩 Module Architecture
1️⃣ Vendor Bandwidth Purchase Management
Features:
Add & manage upstream bandwidth vendors
Record bandwidth purchases:
Capacity (Mbps / Gbps)
Cost (PKR/USD)
Purchase date
Vendor-specific rates
Data Tracking:

✔ Purchase history
✔ Cost per Mbps
✔ Multi-vendor comparison
✔ Contract duration (optional)

2️⃣ Central Bandwidth Pool System
Functionality:
Maintain a virtual bandwidth pool
Aggregate total purchased capacity
Deduct allocated bandwidth dynamically
Metrics:
Total Purchased Bandwidth
Allocated Bandwidth
Available Bandwidth
Utilization %
3️⃣ Reseller Panel & Balance Management
Features:
Create reseller accounts
Assign panel access
Maintain reseller wallet/balance
Key Logic:

✔ Resellers do NOT consume bandwidth directly
✔ They operate using prepaid balance system

4️⃣ Recharge-Based Distribution Model
Workflow:
Reseller sends payment (e.g., PKR 10,000)
Admin credits reseller wallet
Reseller uses balance to sell services
System Tracks:
Total recharge per reseller
Balance usage
Remaining credit
Transaction history
5️⃣ Profit Margin Configuration Engine
Flexible Profit Models:

✔ Fixed margin per Mbps
✔ Percentage-based margin
✔ Package-based pricing override

Dynamic Application:
Profit applied during:
Balance allocation
Package assignment
Service provisioning
6️⃣ Monthly Accounting & Profit/Loss Engine
Automated Monthly Calculation:

System generates:

Total Bandwidth Purchase Cost
Total Reseller Recharge Amount
Total Revenue
Net Profit / Loss
Formula:
Profit = Total Reseller Recharge – Total Bandwidth Purchase Cost
