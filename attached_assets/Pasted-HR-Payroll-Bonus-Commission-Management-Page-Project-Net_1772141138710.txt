HR & Payroll – Bonus & Commission Management Page

Project: NetSphere ISP Billing SaaS

🎯 Objective

Design and build a professional, attractive, enterprise-grade Bonus & Commission Management page.

This page must allow:

Define bonus structures

Configure commission rules

Assign performance-based incentives

Link commission to sales / collections / reseller performance

Integrate with Payroll processing

Provide approval workflow

Maintain audit logs

✔ Fixed bonus
✔ Performance-based bonus
✔ Sales commission
✔ Collection commission
✔ Reseller commission sharing
✔ Department-based commission rules
