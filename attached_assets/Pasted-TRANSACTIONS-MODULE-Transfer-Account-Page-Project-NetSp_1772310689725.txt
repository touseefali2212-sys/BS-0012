TRANSACTIONS MODULE
Transfer Account Page

Project: NetSphere ISP Billing & Enterprise ERP

🎯 Objective

Develop a secure Account Transfer Management Page to manage internal fund transfers between financial accounts.

This page ensures:

✔ Controlled internal fund movement
✔ Real-time balance updates
✔ Double-entry accounting compliance
✔ Branch-level transfer tracking
✔ Cash flow visibility
✔ Audit-safe transaction logging

This is a financial control mechanism — not revenue, but internal liquidity management.

🔄 System Logic

When transfer is processed:

✔ Deduct amount from From Account
✔ Add amount to To Account
✔ Generate double-entry journal

Automatic Journal Entry:

Debit:

Destination Account

Credit:

Source Account

If transfer fee exists:

Debit:

Transfer Fee Expense

Credit:

Bank / Cash

Balances must update instantly.
