NOTIFICATIONS MODULE
Push Notification – Bulk & Campaign Tab Page

Project: NetSphere ISP Billing & Enterprise ERP

🎯 Objective

Develop a dedicated Bulk & Campaign Management Tab under Push Notifications to create, schedule, manage, and analyze large-scale push notification campaigns.

This page ensures:

✔ Mass communication control
✔ Targeted campaign delivery
✔ Customer segmentation
✔ Scheduled broadcast alerts
✔ Engagement tracking
✔ Performance analytics

It transforms push notifications into a structured marketing and operational broadcast system.

🔄 Automation & Smart Logic

Campaign triggers can also be linked to system events:

Examples:

• Send maintenance announcement to specific city
• Notify overdue customers about payment
• Announce new internet packages
• Broadcast service outage alerts
• Internal policy updates to staff

Escalation:

If critical campaign not acknowledged → escalate to supervisor.

Rate Limiting:

Prevents spam by limiting notification frequency per user.

Queue-Based Delivery:

Ensures high-volume campaigns process without system overload.
