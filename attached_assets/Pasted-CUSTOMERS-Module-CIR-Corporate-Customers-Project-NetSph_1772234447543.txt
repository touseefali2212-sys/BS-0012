🏢 PAGE 1: CIR Customers Page Description
________________________________________
🎯 Purpose of CIR Customers
CIR (Committed Information Rate) customers are:
•	Dedicated bandwidth clients
•	SLA-based connections
•	Fixed speed guaranteed
•	Linked directly with Bandwidth Vendor
•	Usually businesses or institutions
This page must manage:
✔ Dedicated bandwidth
✔ Vendor mapping
✔ Static IP
✔ Contract
✔ SLA
✔ Billing cycle
✔ Monitoring

🎯 Purpose of Corporate Customers
Corporate Customers are:
•	Large businesses
•	Multi-branch companies
•	Multiple connections under one account
•	Special billing agreements
•	Custom packages
They may have:
✔ Multiple sites
✔ Multiple bandwidth types
✔ Hybrid services (CIR + Shared)
✔ Centralized billing
