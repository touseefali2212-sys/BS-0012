Reseller Collections Page

Project: NetSphere ISP Billing & Enterprise ERP

🎯 Objective

Develop a dedicated Reseller Collections Management Page to manage wallet top-ups, credit settlements, and payment collections from resellers.

This page ensures:

✔ Controlled reseller wallet funding
✔ Credit recovery tracking
✔ Commission adjustments
✔ Real-time wallet balance updates
✔ Accounting integration
✔ Risk & exposure monitoring

This is a revenue control center for your reseller network.

🔄 System Logic

When collection recorded:

✔ Update wallet balance
✔ Adjust credit outstanding
✔ Update reseller dashboard
✔ Generate accounting entry
✔ Update financial reports
✔ Remove suspension if cleared

If wallet becomes positive:

✔ Restore active status

If credit exceeds limit:

✔ Trigger warning alert
