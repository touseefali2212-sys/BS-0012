HR & Payroll Module – Advance & Loan Management Page

Project: NetSphere ISP Billing SaaS

🎯 Objective

Create a professional, enterprise-grade Advance & Loan Management page under the HR & Payroll module.

This page must allow the company to:

Issue salary advances

Provide employee loans

Track repayment schedules

Auto-deduct installments from payroll

Manage approvals

Maintain financial transparency

Integrate with Accounting module

The design must be clean, structured, attractive, and corporate.

🔄 Payroll Integration Logic

System must:

Automatically deduct installment amount from monthly payroll.

Update remaining balance.

Log transaction in Accounting module.

Generate journal entry automatically.

Journal Entry Example:
Dr: Employee Advance Account
Cr: Cash/Bank Account

💰 Accounting Integration

Every advance/loan must:

Create ledger entry

Appear in financial reports

Create separate chart of account:

Employee Advance Account

Employee Loan Account
