INVENTORY MODULE
Brands & Products Page

Project: NetSphere ISP Billing & Enterprise ERP

🎯 Objective

Develop a centralized Brands & Products Page to manage all inventory items, linked brands, pricing, stock visibility, and operational attributes.

This page controls:

✔ Actual physical products
✔ Brand-level classification
✔ Model management
✔ SKU tracking
✔ Cost & sale pricing
✔ Serial & MAC tracking
✔ Product availability

This page connects Inventory, Assets, Procurement, and Sales.

🔄 Automation Logic

When product created:

✔ Available in Purchase Orders
✔ Available in Sales Module
✔ Available in Assets Module (if enabled)

If serial tracking enabled:

✔ Serial mandatory during GRN entry

If rental enabled:

✔ Available in Rental / Customer Assignment

If stock falls below reorder level:

✔ Trigger low-stock alert
✔ Suggest reorder quantity

If discontinued:

✔ Block new purchase orders
✔ Hide from POS
