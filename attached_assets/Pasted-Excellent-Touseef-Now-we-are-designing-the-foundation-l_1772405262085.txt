When Product Type created:

✔ Available in Inventory Module
✔ Available in Purchase Module
✔ Available in Sales Module
✔ Available in Assets Module (if Asset type)

✔ Serial mandatory during stock entry

If “Depreciation Applicable” enabled:

✔ Auto-link to Asset Depreciation Module

If “Require IP Allocation” enabled:

✔ Auto-link to IPAM during assignment
